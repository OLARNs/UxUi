01 — Foundations

Design Principles

Five principles governing every design decision in REAVER.

01
Precision
Every element is intentional. Each component earns its place through function alone.
02
Trust
Consistent patterns and reliable feedback. Users always know where they are and what will happen next.
03
Speed
Optimized for fast transactions. Minimal clicks, clear CTAs, zero friction between intent and action.
04
Premium
Elevated through restraint — generous spacing, sharp contrast, obsessive consistency.
05
Clarity
Content-first layout. Game artwork provides context; actions are always the visual priority.
